Concept

An attempt at an alternative setting importing Oriental (specifically Japanese) culture into a playable world to be used in conjunction with the rules from the World of Darkness 1st Edition.

Players roll Oriental based characters to play as semi-immortal humans who can change into forms of a Dragon.

Gameplay Changes

Full rules from the World of Darkness with a number of customizations.

  • Blood Pool points are replaced with Gnosis points.
  • Draconis is an additional specialized discipline available to all characters.
  • Dragon clans replaced the traditional vampire clans.
Draconis

Custom and clan specialized discipline.

  • 1 - Dragon Sense - gain an impression of an individual by rolling Per+Drãconis, dif 6; at higher levels allows the drãgon to see extra dimensions, auras, etc
  • 2 - Dragon Claw - hands and feet morph into drãconian-like features; does STR+3 damage, hands and feet have 3 talons - some may grow a tail
  • 3 - CLAN SPECIFIC
  • 4 - Dragon Flight - can grow wings (10ft span) out of shoulders and can fly at a rate of 5 mph, costs 3 gnosis
  • 5 - Draconian Form - transforms into a unique drãgon-humanoid; increases in size 1/2, costs 3 gnosis points(4 w/wings), takes 1 turn to fully transform; STR+3, DEX+2, STA+4, gain extra health level permanant
  • 6 - CLAN SPECIFIC
  • 7 - Dragon Form - transforms in to a large adult dragon of appropriate color; has 15 health levels, STR+5, DEX+6, STA+5, CLAW+3, BITE+4, TAIL+2, 20mph flight speed per drãconis level, 2/3 size increase, +5 to soak, gain 3 health levels
  • 7 - Breath Weapon - emits a charge of the drãgons breath weapon; damage is equal to users health level - electricity, fire, lava, cold, void, water

Each Clan has a unique set of Disciplines, along with specialized Draconis abilities and their own set of weaknesses.

Black Dragons
Drãgons of Darkness and Death
Kuroi-Ryu-Kai
Description

Black Dragons are the stealthy, deadly, and unmerciful warriors of the Clan, and have held this responsibility since the first days of the Clan; the Black Dragons are the founders, students, and enthusiasts of the numerous martial arts found throughout Japan and China; Blacks are as old and as honor bound as the Golds’, and just as powerful, but prefer to let others rule, at least the daily aspects of the Orient; most Blacks are born into old, ninja or samurai families, and are trained for iniation into the order from an early age; Blacks can be classified into three groups: the Ninja, the Kuroi-Neko, and the Ronin (Kuroi-Neko are the thieves, and such who specialize in the non-violent arts of climbing, pilfering, and the like; Ronin are those, who for any reason, don’t have a master; and the Ninja are those skilled in the arts of stealth and assassination); most blacks tend to stay within the communities of their own kind, but welcome and honor the others of their clan, good or evil

most Blacks prefer their clan color to any other, but styles of dress can range from simple kimonos to advanced combat gear
