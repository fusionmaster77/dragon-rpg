Concept

An attempt at an alternative setting importing Oriental (specifically Japanese) culture into a playable world to be used in conjunction with the rules from the Vampire the Masqquerade 1st Edition Players Handbook.

Players roll Oriental based characters to play as semi-immortal humans who can change into forms of a Dragon.

World is populated with all sorts of World of Darkness denziens such as vampires, werewolves, mages, etc..

Gameplay Changes

Full rules from the World of Darkness with a number of customizations.

  • Dragons are not Vampires but use some of the same Disciplines
  • Blood Pool points are replaced with Gnosis points.
  • Draconis is an additional specialized discipline available to all characters.
  • Dragon clans replaced the traditional vampire clans.
Draconis

Custom and clan specialized discipline.

  • 1 - Dragon Sense - gain an impression of an individual by rolling Per+Draconis, dif 6; at higher levels allows the dragon to see extra dimensions, auras, etc
  • 2 - Dragon Claw - hands and feet morph into draconian-like features; does STR+3 damage, hands and feet have 3 talons - some may grow a tail
  • 3 - CLAN SPECIFIC
  • 4 - Dragon Flight - can grow wings (10ft span) out of shoulders and can fly at a rate of 5 mph, costs 3 gnosis
  • 5 - Draconian Form - transforms into a unique dragon-humanoid; increases in size 1/2, costs 3 gnosis points(4 w/wings), takes 1 turn to fully transform; STR+3, DEX+2, STA+4, gain extra health level permanant
  • 6 - CLAN SPECIFIC
  • 7 - Breath Weapon - emits a charge of the dragons breath weapon; damage is equal to users health level - electricity, fire, lava, cold, void, water
  • 8 - Dragon Form - transforms in to a large adult dragon of appropriate color; has 15 health levels, STR+5, DEX+6, STA+5, CLAW+3, BITE+4, TAIL+2, 20mph flight speed per draconis level, 2/3 size increase, +5 to soak, gain 3 health levels

Dragon Breeds

The breeds of the dragons are two: homid, and dragon. They mix freely and get along quite well, but there is that air of superiority emenating from the dragon breed, as they hold many of the most influential and powerful positions. The dragons have six clans: black, blue, red, white, gold, and green. Each clan is profoundly different and usually stay to their own, but are honored when gived the chance to mix with others colors. All dragons are of Japanese stock and are prepared for the honor when they are very young. Some families of dragons are considered reverant and are indeed groomed for positions of power.

  • HOMID - born of a human parent, beginning Gnosis 3
  • DRAGON - born of a dragon parent, beginning Gnosis 5
Dragon Forms

There are three forms of the dragon: homid, draconian, and dragon. Each form takes 1 full round to transform into, after rolling STA+Primal Urge, difficulty of the form. A dragon of homid breed has a 5 difficulty to transform into homid form, likewise for those born a dragon.

  • HOMID - no trait adjustment
  • DRACONIAN - This form is a mixture of man and dragon, and is often quite horrible and terrifying to look at. Each form is unique and can be modified by spending time meditating and practicing. STR+3, DEX+2, STA+3, PER+2; DIFF 7
  • DRAGON - This form is also unique and varies greatly from dragon to dragon, from the small, fast scouts, to the huge, imposing clan leaders. Size depends on rank, each dragon increses by 1/8 for each level of rank. STR+5, DEX+6, STA+5, CLAW+3, BITE+4, TAIL+2, can fly 20mph per rank, and gets +5 to soak.
Dragon Language

The dragon language is old and complicated, and its secrets are closely guarded. The written language is a mixture of Japanes style ideograms, and ancient dragons runes, and the spoken language is even moreso misunderstood, and hard to learn. They also have a language for the nungen,or mortals, which incorporates some characters from this language.

Description

Black Dragons are the stealthy, deadly, and unmerciful warriors of the Clan, and have held this responsibility since the first days of the Clan; the Black Dragons are the founders, students, and enthusiasts of the numerous martial arts found throughout Japan and China; Blacks are as old and as honor bound as the Golds’, and just as powerful, but prefer to let others rule, at least the daily aspects of the Orient; most Blacks are born into old, ninja or samurai families, and are trained for iniation into the order from an early age; Blacks can be classified into three groups: the Ninja, the Kuroi-Neko, and the Ronin (Kuroi-Neko are the thieves, and such who specialize in the non-violent arts of climbing, pilfering, and the like; Ronin are those, who for any reason, don’t have a master; and the Ninja are those skilled in the arts of stealth and assassination); most blacks tend to stay within the communities of their own kind, but welcome and honor the others of their clan, good or evil
